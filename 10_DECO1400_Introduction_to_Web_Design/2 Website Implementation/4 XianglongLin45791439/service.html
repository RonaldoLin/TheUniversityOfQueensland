FLASH FREE
- Shuttle Services

Whether you come to our resort for vacation or just visit, we are happy to provide you with Time-limited Free shuttle service including various forms (“Triphibian: sea, land & air”), Now or Never!

  • Sea

    If you come to the resort through water transportation, we can send a yacht or a sailboat or other form of transportation to the port for pickup.

  • Land

    We also provide a bus or a car to the train station for pickup.

  • Air

    For pickup to the flight, a bus or a car will be sent to the airport as well.
